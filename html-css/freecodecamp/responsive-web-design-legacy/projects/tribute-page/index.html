David Lynch

The man who catches the biggest fish.

Fun facts about David Lynch:

  • David Lynch was born on Sunday, January 20th, 1946.
  • Lynch was at one point married to Mary Fisk, sister of long-time collaborator and legendary production designer Jack Fisk. Lynch and Fisk were childhood friends.
  • During the production of Eraserhead, Lynch spent a period literally living on the set as he worked on the film. Talk about hands-on!
